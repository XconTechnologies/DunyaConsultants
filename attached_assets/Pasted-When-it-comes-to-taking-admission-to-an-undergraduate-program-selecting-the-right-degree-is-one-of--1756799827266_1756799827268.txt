What is Bachelor Degree in Pakistan?
In Pakistan, a bachelor’s degree is an educational qualification that can take around 3-5 years to complete, depending on the program and university. The duration of the program is also dependent on whether it is an honors or a professional bachelor’s degree.

An honors bachelor’s degree will typically take around three years. However, sometimes it can take 4 years to complete.
A professional bachelor’s degree in Pakistan normally takes 4-5 years to complete.
